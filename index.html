Privacy Policy

MJB48 Football Journey

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

MJB48 Football Journey ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal football training journal app. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

The app may access the following on your device:

  • Camera: To take photos for your Player Card profile picture
  • Photo Library: To select photos and videos for your profile and video gallery
  • Storage: To save your training data, match logs, and settings locally on your device

How We Use Your Information

All data you enter into the app (matches, training sessions, videos, photos, stats) is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Collect or transmit your personal data to any servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or advertising services

Data Storage

All your data remains on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted. We recommend backing up any important photos or videos separately.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by young football players and their families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data stays on the device and is controlled by the user.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can delete any data within the app or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
